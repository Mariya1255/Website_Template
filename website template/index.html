Delicious French Toast

A thick slice of French toast topped with vanilla ice cream, crunchy roasted hazelnuts, and served with a side of syrup for a perfect sweet breakfast treat.

Price: $8.99

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices thick bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Ice cream, hazelnuts, and syrup (for topping)

Instructions:

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, and vanilla extract together in a bowl.
  2. Dip bread slices into the mixture until fully soaked.
  3. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat and cook bread slices until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Top with ice cream, hazelnuts, and syrup. Serve immediately.

Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish with creamy sauce.

Price: $12.50

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, chopped
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and Parmesan cheese together in a bowl.
  4. Drain spaghetti and mix it with the pancetta, then quickly stir in the egg mixture.
  5. Season with black pepper and serve hot.

Avocado Toast

A healthy and delicious breakfast option.

Price: $6.00

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices bread
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, red pepper flakes, olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread slices to desired crispness.
  2. Mash avocado in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Spread mashed avocado on toasted bread and add desired toppings.
  4. Serve immediately.
